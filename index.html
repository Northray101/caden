What Caden is

Your home, always aware. Caden runs continuously in the background, monitors the things you care about, remembers your context, and decides on its own when something is worth telling you. No app to open. No question to ask first.

How it works

One orchestrator. A pool of specialised agents. A News Monitor scans every fifteen minutes. A Self-Check agent runs hourly. A Repair agent audits from a second machine. They report to the brain, the brain decides, and the brain reaches you — through the dashboard, a kiosk, or a message.
